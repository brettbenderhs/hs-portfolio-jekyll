Why do LEDs matter to me?

LEDs are used almost everywhere, including phones, game consoles, and more!

Where are they used in my phone?

They are used all over the place, from your flash, to the screen, and even inside!

Okay, so what elements are involved?

There are a ton of elements involved, including Gallium, which this page will be focusing on!

What even is gallium?

Reactivity

Melting Temp

Where is it used in LEDs?

The light producing semiconductor

Other uses?

Some medical thermometers, mirrors.

What does it look like?
